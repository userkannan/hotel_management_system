About Us

Welcome to HOTEL KANNAN, your premier destination for luxury and comfort. Nestled in the heart of Tiruvannamalai, our hotel offers an unparalleled experience that combines modern amenities with timeless elegance. Whether you're visiting for business or leisure, we strive to make your stay unforgettable.

At HOTEL KANNAN, we pride ourselves on our exceptional service and attention to detail. Our well-appointed rooms and suites are designed to provide you with the utmost in comfort and convenience. Each room features plush bedding, high-speed internet, flat-screen TVs, and stunning views of the city or surrounding landscape. Our dedicated staff is committed to ensuring that your needs are met, from the moment you check in to the time you depart.

At HOTEL KANNAN, we believe in creating memorable experiences for our guests. Our commitment to excellence and our passion for hospitality set us apart. We invite you to discover the charm and elegance of our hotel and look forward to welcoming you soon.

Our hotel also boasts a range of facilities to enhance your stay, including a state-of-the-art fitness center, a rejuvenating spa, and a sparkling swimming pool. For those planning events, our versatile meeting and banquet spaces are perfect for conferences, weddings, and social gatherings.

Indulge in a culinary journey at our on-site restaurant, where our talented chefs create delectable dishes using the finest local ingredients. For a more relaxed setting, enjoy a refreshing cocktail or a cup of coffee at our cozy lounge or terrace.
